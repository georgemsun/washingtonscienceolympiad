Board Nominations

Interested in becoming a member of the state board?

WSO is always looking for hardworking, dedicated individuals to be on the WSO Board of Directors. Fill out the nomination form (PDF/DOCX) and return to the State Director. Nominations generally close by April each year. Self-nominations are accepted.
